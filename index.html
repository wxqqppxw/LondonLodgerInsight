Housing Insight

Starting your first steps in London！

Every year, millions of international students come to London for their study, but few of them could live in the apartments provided by colleges. 

That’s why we are addressing the challenge that international students face when looking to rent accommodations in London. This includes finding locations that meet their needs in terms of amenities, transportation options, and neighborhood characteristics. Our project aims to aid these students in making informed decisions about where to live in London. We provide visual representations of relevant factors to simplify the process of evaluating the environment near rental properties.

Additionally, we'll offer a feature for students to rate their previous accommodations, contributing to a pool of reviews that can guide future tenants. This initiative seeks to enhance their living experience in the city by providing a comprehensive view of what to expect from different areas and dwellings.
